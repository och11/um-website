Academics

Eleven Colleges. Infinite Possibilities.

The University of Miami offers more than 180 undergraduate degree programs across eleven colleges and schools. Whether you're drawn to marine biology, law, music performance, or data science, UM provides an academic environment shaped by groundbreaking research and passionate faculty.

The Rosenstiel School of Marine, Atmospheric, and Earth Science stands as a global leader in ocean research, with access to the waters of Biscayne Bay right at our doorstep. The Frost School of Music produces Grammy Award winners. The Miller School of Medicine consistently ranks among the nation's best.

UM's small class sizes — averaging just 22 students — mean professors know your name, your ambitions, and your potential. Real mentorship. Real research. Real impact.

Athletics

Five National Championships. One Family.

The Miami Hurricanes are one of the most storied programs in college football history, with five national championships and a legacy of producing NFL superstars like Ray Lewis, Warren Sapp, and Ed Reed. But at UM, sports are more than wins and losses — they're a community experience.

Across 17 NCAA Division I sports, UM athletes compete at the highest levels while balancing rigorous academic demands. The Hurricanes compete in the ACC and have produced more than 300 NFL draft picks over the decades. On any given game day, the entire campus comes alive in orange, green, and white.

Beyond football, UM excels in baseball (home to multiple College World Series appearances), swimming, tennis, golf, and soccer. Every sport reflects the Hurricanes' relentless drive to compete and win.
