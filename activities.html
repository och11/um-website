At the University of Miami, life doesn't stop when class ends. With more than 200 registered student organizations, clubs, and groups, every interest has a home. Whether you want to lead, serve, create, compete, or connect, there's a community waiting for you.

Student government, cultural organizations, professional fraternities, honor societies, performance groups, and volunteer corps all thrive at UM — supported by the Toppel Career Center and the Division of Student Affairs.

Living at UM

Campus Life That Matches Miami's Energy

University of Miami's residential experience is second to none. With on-campus housing for more than 5,000 students and a suite of world-class facilities, the campus itself becomes a destination.

The iconic Lake Osceola serves as the heart of campus — a gathering place for students to study, relax, kayak, or simply soak up the South Florida sunshine. Surrounded by lush tropical landscaping and architecturally stunning buildings, UM's campus is routinely ranked among the most beautiful in the country.
