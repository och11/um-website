About UM

A Private Research University Like No Other

Founded in 1925, the University of Miami has grown into one of the nation's premier private research universities, with more than 17,000 students from around the globe. Set in Coral Gables — one of America's most beautiful planned cities — UM offers a campus experience that blends tropical beauty with intellectual rigor.

From the Frost School of Music to the Miller School of Medicine, UM's eleven colleges and schools provide students with unparalleled opportunities to learn, discover, and create. Our close-knit community fosters collaboration across disciplines, producing graduates who lead in every field imaginable.
